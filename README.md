# READ ME!
You will need an sd card for it to work! So grab a one yourself.

# Downloading and watching videos
In CardTube, you can download videos. First, go to [SETTINGS] tab and choose your Wi-Fi. If you have a password, type it in, and then click [ CONNECT ].

This will allow you to download videos. Now go to [ONLINE] tab and choose a video. Those videos are my test videos. If you want your video, just message me on my discord @Filipo3221

If you click on a video, it will start downloading. Wait until it finishes. REMINDER! I DIDN'T FULLY FINISH DOWNLOADING VIDEOS WITH .wav SOUND FILES! IT WILL BE ONLY THE VIDEO!

When the video downloaded, you can watch it everywhere at any time! It is downloaded on an SD card. To watch it, just change the tab to [SD CARD].

That's how you download videos and play them!
